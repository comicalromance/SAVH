Task 3: Starting a New Conversation
Part 1: Starting a New Conversation
To start a new conversation, tap on the green "New Chat Button" at the bottom right of the screen. Then, double tap the screen.
Part 2: The Contacts Page
Double tapping the screen will bring you to the contacts page, which displays a list of all your contacts. Select a contact from the list by swiping left/right or tapping, and then double tap the screen to confirm.
Part 3: Writing the Message
Once you have selected a contact, you will be immediately brought to the conversation page, with the message input box automatically focused like before. Similar to before, double tap the screen to display the keyboard and type the message you desire. Once done, swipe to, or tap the "Send" Button, and double tap the screen.
