Connecting to Device

Task 2: Managing Conversations
Part 1: Entering a Conversation
To reply to a conversation, first select a conversation on the main "Chat" page of Whatsapp by tapping on it, or swiping left/right to it. Double tapping the screen after selecting it will lead you to the conversation page.
Part 2: Reading through a Conversation
The conversation page will display all the messages sent and received within that group or contact. Read these messages by tapping on them, or swiping left/right to them. By default, the message box is on focus. Swiping left will allow you to read messages, starting from the most recent message.
Part 3: Replying to a Conversation (I)
By default, upon entering a conversation, the message input box at the bottom is automatically set on focus. If the message input box is not focused, simply swipe right until you hear "Type a message", or tap near the bottom of the screen.
Part 4: Replying to a Conversation (II)
Once the focus is on the message input box, double tapping the screen will display the keyboard, where you can type your message. After typing the message, swipe right until you hear "Send Button", or tap on the Green Send button, and double tap the screen. A "plop" sound will confirm that the message has been sent.
