Connecting to Device

Task 2: Scrolling
Part 1: Two-finger scrolling
To scroll through a vertictal list, find a list item, then move two fingers up or down. To scroll through a horizontal list, find a list item, then move two fingers left or right.
Part 2: Scrolling shortcuts
You can jump forward or backward in a list using a shortcut. To jump forward, move one finger right then left in a single motion. To jump back, move one finger left then right. Give it a try here.
Part 3: Practice
Scroll by sliding two fingers up or down. Jump forward by moving one finger right then left. Jump back by moving one figher left then right.
