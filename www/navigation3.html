Connecting to Device

Task 3: TalkBack menus
Part 1: Global TalkBack menu
TalkBack has two different menus. The first, the Global TalkBack menu, always contains the same commands. You can activate it by moving a single finger down then right in a single motion, then raising your finger. Activate a practice version now.
Part 2: Local TalkBack menu The second menu is the Local TalkBack menu. The items in this menu are different depending on what you last heard. You can activate this menu by moving a single finger up and then right in a single motion, then raising your finger. ACtivate a practice menu now.
Part 3: Practice Move your finger donw then right to activate the Global TalkBack menu. Move your finger up then right to activate the Local TalkBack menu.
