Connecting to Device

Task 1: The Whatsapp Application
Part 1: Finding Whatsapp
Whatsapp is an instant messaging application that enables smartphone users to communicate for free, as long as they have internet connection. This application is available for download on the Google Play Store. Once downloaded, you can access the application by tapping on the Whatsapp icon, and then double tapping the screen.
Part 2: The Whatsapp Interface
Upon opening the application, the main page of Whatsapp will be displayed. The main page contains a list of all the messages received or sent, organized by conversations with individuals or groups.
Part 3: Navigating Whatsapp Messages
You can either tap or swipe right/left to select a conversation - upon selecting a conversation, Talkback will read out the name of the contact or group, and the content of the last message. You can also scroll through the list of conversations by moving two fingers up or down the screen.
